Oh The Biomes You'll Go

Oh The biomes You'll Go is one of the best mods for world generation, I'd even say this is the best one. It adds over 80 new biomes, over 40 unique trees and a lot more things! The biomes are perfect for magic / adventure modpack because it creates a beautiful atmosphere for this type of gameplay.

Summary of changes:

  1. Over 40 unique trees
  2. Over 28 wood types
  3. Over 200 new blocks and items
  4. New ores, armor and tools!

Supported versions:

  • 1.16.5
  • 1.16.4
  • 1.16.3
  • 1.16.2
  • 1.16.1
  • 1.15.2
