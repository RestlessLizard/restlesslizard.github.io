Savage & Ravage

Savage & Ravage is a mod that overhauls illagers, making them deadlier and much more interesting.

Summary of changes:

  1. Bad omen effect not won't apply on killing the Raid Captain
  2. To get bad omen burn the illager banner with flint and steel
  3. Improved pillager outposts
  4. Pillagers may spawn with Firework Rocket, that will be used as a weapon against you
  5. New textures for Vindicators, Evokers and Vexes
  6. Creepers were overhauled:
    • Their explosion no longer destroys block
    • The explosion creates a bunch of Creepies
    • Creepies are small creepers that create a small explosion that does minor damage

Supported versions:

  • 1.16.4
  • 1.16.1
  • 1.15.2
