Create

Create is a technical mod that provides you with ability to create (no pun intended) beautiful machines interconnected with one another. The name of the mod is a pretty good explanation of what you can do with it, because possibilities are close to endless. From automating resource smelting to creating machines that mine for you automatically placing rails to go forward, from small autofarms of crops to huge tree farms that operate on coal they are generating from the trees they are chopping down. Even elevators, automatic doors, planes, trains and much more.

What mod adds:

  1. New materials
    • Copper
    • Zinc
    • Brass
    • Rose quartz
  2. Kinetic power
  3. Waterwheels, windmills, cogwheels
  4. Item filers, mechanical belts, fluid pipes and tanks
  5. Mechanical drill, saw, harvester
  6. Bearings, gearshift, clutch, gearboxes, shafts
  7. Press, mixer, basin, depot
  8. Speedometer, stressometer
  9. Latches, repeaters, redstone link, lever with strength control

Supported versions:

  • 1.16.5
  • 1.16.4
  • 1.15.2
  • 1.14.4
